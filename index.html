Sayambhu

Swayambhu, also known as the Monkey Temple, is one of the oldest and most sacred Buddhist sites in Nepal. It sits on a hilltop and offers a beautiful view of Kathmandu Valley. The stupa represents peace, wisdom, and harmony, and people visit it to pray, meditate, and enjoy the peaceful environment.

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath Temple is one of the most important Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located on the banks of the Bagmati River in Kathmandu. Thousands of pilgrims visit this temple for blessings, especially during Maha Shivaratri. The temple is known for its holy rituals, spiritual energy, and cultural significance.
